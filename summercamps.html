Summer Camp
The summer camp offering is unique and in high demand for those looking to get a jump start on how to take advantage of the natural beauty and offerings of Narragansett Bay and our shoreline.
Camp Information
Who:   Children ages 8-16

When:   June 15, 2020 through August 28, 2020
      Monday through Friday, 6 hours per day
      Options: 6:00 am to 12:00 pm, 7:00 am to 1:00 pm, 8:00 am to 2:00 PM

Where:  Drop off at Safe Harbor Marina - (Brewer's South Yard), 1 Mast Head Drive, Warwick RI 02886

*Cost per individual: $850
*Maximum camp size is 5
What You Will Experience
  • - See different parts of the Bay each day from the northern most point to the ocean coastline
  • - Stop at each lighthouse
  • - Catching bait ~ crabs, eels, baitfish
  • - Fishing ~ basic to advanced, fluke, sea bass, scup, bluefish, stripers
  • - Clamming from shore
  • - Lobstering ~ pulling pots and baiting
  • - Snailing ~ pulling pots and baiting
  • - Tour of an island (Hope, Dutch, or Patience)
  • - Visit an oyster farm ~ harvesting
  • - Tagging fish
  • - Review state regulations
  • - Basic boating navigation
  • - Use of boat electronics
  • - Sell fish caught at Galilee wholesaler
  • - Plenty of fun!


Schedule
  • Upper Bay
    Northern tip of Prudence Island to The Providence River and Port
    Activities: basic boating safety, tour of bay, fishing, clamming
    Day 1
  • Mid Bay
    Patience, Prudence, Hog and Hope Island
    Activities: basic boating navigation and electronics, tour of bay, fishing, sailing
    Day 2
  • West Bay
    Wickford, Jamestown, Dutch Island, Bonnet Shores and Beavertail
    Activities: tour of bay, tagging and release fish program, fishing, quahogging
    Day 3
  • East Bay
    Jamestown Harbor, Newport Harbor, Castle Hill and Brenton Reef
    Activities: tour of bay, fishing
    Day 4
  • Point Judith
    Narragansett, Galilee, Harbor of Refuge and Scarborough
    Activities: boat tour, fishing, selling fish
    Day 5
