About Tails Up
Are you taking full advantage of Narragansett Bay and the surrounding Atlantic Ocean shoreline? Well if not, Tails Up is here to be your “Salt Water Connection.” Whether you are a novice or advanced around the salt water, we have something for you. Rhode Island is comprised of over 400 miles of saltwater coastline and it’s a great natural resource that offers plenty to explore year round. So don’t miss out and expedite your knowledge with our services. Explore our website and try one or more of the offerings for an experience that will last. A great customer experience is our ultimate goal, and we are always open to discussing program modifications and special requests.
