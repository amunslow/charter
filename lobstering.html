Did you know?
Did you know that as a resident you could possess five lobster pots for your personal use? The lobster industry in RI is changing due to global warming, so before it disappears take advantage of fresh lobster summer through fall.


What you will learn
Over a four-hour charter as we bait and pull traps, you will learn state regulations and licensing, gear required and where to buy, bait options and what works best, where to drop pots, how to pull traps, and how to care for lobsters.
